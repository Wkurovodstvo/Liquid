Dota2
The International 8 arrives!

The International, often abbreviated as TI, is an annual tournament organized by Valve. In 2015 Valve increased their number of hosted events, founding the Dota Major Championships which incorporates The International into its structure. The International remains the biggest and most prestigious event on the Major Championship circuit.

EPICENTER XL

The third installment of EPICENTER is a tournament by Epic Esports Events that will be held in Moscow from April 27th to May 6th. The main event will be held at the VTB Ice Palace on May 4–6.

CS:GO
DreamHack Masters Marseille 2018

DreamHack Masters Marseille is the first Masters tournament of 2018, this time visiting Marseille, France. 16 Teams from around the world are in attendance, fighting over their share of the $250,000 USD prize pool in the standard DreamHack Masters format. The event is also part of the first season of the Intel Grand Slam.

November 25 2017

CS:GO
ESL One: Cologne 2018

ESL One: Cologne 2018 is the fifth time ESL's headline event returns to Germany, once again in the Lanxess-Arena, with 16 teams competing for a share of the $300,000 USD prize pool as well as a the opportunity of another tournament win towards the first season of the Intel Grand Slam.

April 14 2018
